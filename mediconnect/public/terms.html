By accessing and using MediConnect ("the Application"), you agree to be bound by these Terms and Conditions. This application is designed for emergency medical technicians (EMTs) and healthcare providers to access patient medical records via FHIR (Fast Healthcare Interoperability Resources) protocol.

2. Development and Testing Purpose

This application is currently in development and testing phase. It is intended for demonstration and educational purposes using Epic's sandbox environment. Do not use this application for actual patient care or with real patient data.

3. Data Privacy and Security

MediConnect accesses your health information through secure SMART on FHIR OAuth 2.0 authentication. We are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the security of your health information in accordance with HIPAA regulations and applicable privacy laws.

  • Your credentials are never stored by MediConnect
  • All data transmission occurs over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Access tokens are securely managed and expire after the session
  • We only request the minimum necessary scopes to provide emergency medical services

4. User Responsibilities

As a user of MediConnect, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate authentication credentials

  • Use the application only for authorized medical purposes

  • Report any security concerns or unauthorized access immediately

This application is authorized for use by:

  • Emergency Medical Technicians (EMTs) providing patient care
