🔒 Trust Self-Signed SSL Certificate

⚠️ Why is this happening?
Your browser doesn't trust the self-signed SSL certificate used for local development. This is normal and expected for HTTPS in development.

Quick Fix:

Step 1: Click this link to open the backend directly:
👉 Open https://localhost:8000/docs
Step 2: Your browser will show a security warning. Click:
  • Chrome/Edge: Click "Advanced" → "Proceed to localhost (unsafe)"
  • Safari: Click "Show Details" → "visit this website"
  • Firefox: Click "Advanced" → "Accept the Risk and Continue"
Step 3: You should see the FastAPI documentation page
Step 4: Close that tab and return to your application
The certificate is now trusted for this session!

After completing these steps:

Go back to your application and refresh the page. The "Failed to fetch" error should be gone!
