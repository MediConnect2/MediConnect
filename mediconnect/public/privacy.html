MediConnect - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 17, 2025

1. Introduction

MediConnect ("we", "our", or "the Application") is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of your health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal health information when you use our FHIR-based medical records access application.

2. Information We Access

Through your authorization, MediConnect accesses the following types of health information from your Electronic Health Record (EHR) system:

  • Patient Demographics: Name, date of birth, gender, contact information, address
  • Medical Conditions: Current and past diagnoses, problem lists
  • Allergies: Drug allergies, food allergies, and other intolerances
  • Medications: Current medication list and prescription history
  • Vital Signs: Blood pressure, heart rate, temperature, oxygen saturation
  • Procedures: Medical procedures and surgical history
  • Immunizations: Vaccination records

3. How We Use Your Information

We access your health information solely for the purpose of providing emergency medical services and facilitating patient care. Specifically:

  • To display your medical information to authorized EMTs and healthcare providers
  • To enable quick access to critical medical data during emergency situations
  • To support informed medical decision-making by emergency responders

4. Data Storage and Retention

Important: MediConnect does NOT permanently store your health information.

  • Your health data is retrieved in real-time from your EHR system when accessed
  • Temporary session data (access tokens) are stored only for the duration of your active session
  • All session data is automatically deleted when you log out or your session expires
  • We do not maintain a database of patient health records

5. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • OAuth 2.0: Secure authentication through SMART on FHIR protocol
  • No Password Storage: We never store your EHR login credentials
  • Session Management: Automatic session timeout and secure token handling
  • Access Controls: Only authorized healthcare providers can access the application

6. HIPAA Compliance

MediConnect is designed to comply with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) and the Health Information Technology for Economic and Clinical Health (HITECH) Act. We implement appropriate administrative, physical, and technical safeguards to protect your Protected Health Information (PHI).

7. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your health information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To prevent serious harm or ensure public safety
  • With your explicit authorization

Your information is only accessible to authorized EMTs and healthcare providers who are actively involved in your care.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your health information:

  • Right to Access: You can review what information MediConnect accesses
  • Right to Revoke: You may revoke MediConnect's access at any time through your EHR patient portal
  • Right to Restrict: You can limit which scopes/data types the application can access
  • Right to Request Corrections: Contact your healthcare provider to correct inaccurate information in your EHR

9. Third-Party Services

MediConnect integrates with:

  • Epic FHIR Server: To retrieve your health information (subject to Epic's privacy policies)
  • OAuth Provider: For secure authentication (Epic's OAuth service)

Please review the privacy policies of these third-party services for information on how they handle your data.

10. Development and Testing

Note: This application is currently in development and uses Epic's sandbox environment for testing. During this phase:

  • Only synthetic (test) patient data should be used
  • Real patient information should never be entered into the sandbox environment
  • This application is for demonstration and educational purposes only

11. Cookies and Tracking

MediConnect uses session cookies solely for:

  • Maintaining your authenticated session
  • Storing temporary OAuth state information
  • Ensuring secure communication between your browser and our servers

We do NOT use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or advertising cookies.

12. Children's Privacy

MediConnect is designed for use by healthcare providers and emergency medical personnel. The application is not intended for direct use by children under 13 years of age.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the application after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

15. Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your health information, we will:

  • Notify you within 60 days of discovering the breach
  • Report the breach to appropriate authorities as required by law
  • Take immediate steps to mitigate any harm
  • Implement additional safeguards to prevent future breaches
